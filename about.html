Jon Wigginton, MD

Jon Wigginton, MD is Portfolio Chief Medical Officer, Cullinan Oncology and Advisor, MPM Capital in Boston, MA. Jon previously served as the Chief Medical Officer and Senior Vice President of Clinical Development at MacroGenics, where he led the establishment of a fully integrated, immuno-oncology clinical development organization, clinical translation of 10 NMEs, execution of a Phase 3 registrational program, and the design and initiation of additional registration-directed programs. Prior to MacroGenics, Dr. Wigginton served as Therapeutic Area Head of Immuno-Oncology, Early Clinical Research at Bristol-Myers Squibb (BMS), where he led the early clinical development strategy and execution of the BMS Immuno-Oncology portfolio including anti-PD-1, anti-PD-L1 and various immunotherapy combinations including anti-CTLA-4/anti-PD-1. These efforts identified multiple clinical signals that led subsequently to registrational trials and regulatory approvals in patients with melanoma, lung, kidney and liver cancers, as well as Hodgkinâ€™s disease, and several seminal publications in the New England Journal of Medicine.

Jon initially joined industry at Merck Research Labs (MRL), where he led various early and late-stage clinical programs. Prior to Merck, Dr. Wigginton was Head of the Investigational Biologics Section in the Center for Cancer Research, National Cancer Institute (NCI-CCR), where he led an integrated in vivo and in vitro basic science program and early clinical research effort focused on combination immunotherapy. Dr. Wigginton also served previously as President of the Society for Immunotherapy of Cancer (SITC), where he led multiple initiatives in the field of cancer immunotherapy. He received his B.S. in Biology and his M.D. from the University of Michigan.

Areas of expertise: oncology, immuno-oncology, global clinical development (Phase 1-3), translational research, regulatory interactions, strategy and filings, due diligence (basic, translational and clinical), strategic advisory support in the setting of advisory boards, board of directors, joint development committees across US, Europe and Asia-Pacific.
